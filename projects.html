SmartStream AI is a GPT-4o level Multimodal Large Language Model (MLLM) designed for vision, speech, and multimodal live streaming on mobile devices. Quantum Computing Simulator

This project would build a classical simulation of quantum algorithms, giving developers and learners the ability to simulate quantum computing processes on their traditional devices. It provides an interface to explore key quantum algorithms (like Grover’s and Shor’s algorithm) without requiring access to actual quantum hardware.
